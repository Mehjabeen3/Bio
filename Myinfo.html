Hobbies

Watching Movies

I strongly believe that movies do effect our lives. They are an excellent source of wonderful stories. They can portray before our eyes the tales of the history of an era or a civilization long gone away, the chronicles of a British spy or the adventures of a little kid who wants to become a great magician like his parents and much more. They can bring back adorable memories from the past. They can encourage us with their brilliant dialogues. Certain films which try to project the reality can make us aware of our surroundings. Movies are an excellent stress-buster when we need a break from a busy schedule.

Travelling

Travelling is the most beautiful and unique experience that everyone must experience in their life. When you travel, you get to visit new places and experience a completely different lifestyle and culture. You have to adopt that lifestyle and culture for a short period, but you learn and experience things you have never done before. It proves to be the best idea for mental peace and the best part is you are going to make a lot of memories that will last forever.

  2. Rangamati

  4. Keokradong

  6. Shat Gambuj
  7. Komoldoho
